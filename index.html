About Triple A Imports

A Belize-based partner for imported consumer products.

Triple A Imports is a Belize-based import and distribution company focused on bringing trusted consumer products to the local market. The company works with Mexican, Central American, Caribbean and selected international products, helping businesses access quality brands through reliable wholesale distribution.

With experience in regional import and distribution, Triple A Imports supports commercial clients with product sourcing, supply coordination and wholesale availability for the Belize market.

Leadership note

The company has been publicly associated with Amir Carrillo, a business figure with experience in import and distribution across the region.
